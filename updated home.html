Welcome to Autistic Inclusion

This space is autistic-informed, neurodiversity-affirming, and grounded in care. It’s for families, educators, therapists, and allies who want to better understand and support autistic children through perspectives led by autistic people—not systems that pathologize difference.

We focus on language, identity, empowerment, and inclusive practice—especially in early education and therapy settings. You’ll find ideas, reframes, and reflections here that invite you to unlearn deficit-based frameworks and engage with autism through respect, relationship, and curiosity.

Rooted in care, this space is for unlearning and reimagining.
